《Summary》	
	At a mountain road behind the school, a bus stop that went out of service twenty years ago, 【Amayado】―――
	where bullied girl ”Chitose” meets a girl ”Kobato” waiting for a bus that shouldn't ever come・・・

	※This is a work of fiction.
	There is no relation to any real person, organization, or event.

《Play time》
	2, 3 hours
